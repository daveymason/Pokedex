Notes:

Made by David Mason. This app acts like a pokedex so people can see basic information about their pokemon. To enhance the user experience, the background image for the panel is giving the illusion of Pikachu "push"ing the panel against the main page. The slide transition from the pokedex to the pokemon page has been slowed down to further enhance the idea that the app is wider than it really is. All Pokemon images are from Serebii.net
